Teriyaki Chicken

Chicken cooked in teriyaki sauce served with broccoli and sticky rice

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken
  • Panda Express mandarin teriyaki sauce
  • Bibligo microwave sticky rice
  • Broccoli

Preparation

  1. Thoroughly puncture the chicken with a fork to tenderize
  2. Slice chicken into bite sized pieces

Cooking

  1. Coat the surface of a pan with vegetable oil or alternative
  2. Heat pan on medium and add chicken, flipping often
  3. Add 1 cup of Broccoli to a bowl, cover bowl with a damp paper towel and microwave for 2 minutes
  4. When chicken is almost fully cooked, add teriyaki sauce and cook for 2 additional minutes, stirring often
  5. Microwave sticky rice
  6. Serve with the chicken and broccoli on top of the sticky rice
